About Safarix

Safarix was created with a passion for travel and a deep love for Africa’s natural beauty. Our mission is to provide unforgettable safari experiences that blend luxury, comfort, and adventure.

We partner with top lodges and expert guides to ensure every journey is safe, immersive, and truly inspiring.
